To add a gift to gifts collection,
put the following code into a new mongo document. 

NOTES:
- The first 2 properties must stay the same.


TROUGH MongoHQ (add new document):

{
 "_id": ObjectId().str,
 "createdAt": new Date(),

 "city": "utrecht",
 "title": "co-working — 1 day",
 "websiteUrl": "http://www.la-cordee.net",
 "websiteName": "@ La cordée",
 "description": "Valid in the office space of your choice."
}



TROUGH mongo console:
1. open another terminal window while meteor is running
2. voer het volgende commando uit: meteor mongo
3. plak onderstaande insert statement (met juiste content)

  db.gifts.insert({
    "_id": ObjectId().str,
    "createdAt": new Date(),
    "city": "utrecht",
    "title": "co-working — 1 day",
    "websiteUrl": "http://www.la-cordee.net",
    "websiteName": "@ La cordée",
    "description": "Valid in the office space of your choice."
  });
